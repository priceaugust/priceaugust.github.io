Meet The Team

We have 2+ years and thousands of hours of experience between us!

August (Gus) Price

Founder

David Young

Founder

Gus is a Systems Engineering student minoring in Finance, and David is a Biomedical Engineering and Computer Science student. They are both Seniors at Washington University in St. Louis. Apart from computers, Gus has extensive background in small electronics and phone repair while David’s expertise is in fabrication, automotive mechanics and electronic tinkering. Both have built computer systems from mild to wild and are up to date on every angle of the computer component market. Their current PC consultation, assembly, and tuning service is founded on a love of computers and nothing more. "We are not out to make a large profit. We have simply found a way to fund our insatiable love of tinkering, and we thank our customers greatly for that." Maybe you've never opened up a computer case before or maybe you’ve been out of the PC scene for a while and want a briefing from a solid pair of brains. Either way, David and Gus are the guys to talk to. The PC market changes so quickly that parts soon become obsolete and a wrong component choice can bottleneck your system. It takes a constant bulky amount of research and experience to stay up to date. No matter your needs (PC-related), David and Gus are happy to meet for a consultation to discuss your options and offer any help they can.
